Help Page

Here you can find a full list of this site's functionality.

1. We want to know you...

It is a little funny element, which can print out your name in the greeting part of the page, if you wrote it in the form. Screenshot (it becomes larger if you move mouse on it):

However, it's not necessary to answer this question. If you want to keep your name in secret, just press "OK" with blank form! Result:

2. Slider

JavaScript controls its moving. Slides are changing with a 3-second interval. But it is not the only thing that can do this slider.

2.1 Move!
2.2 Pause it!

If you click on the current image, slider will stop and appropriate paragraf will open.

2.3 Resume it!

If you click on this image again, slider will resume and paragraf will close.

2.4 A little bit of CSS!

If you move your mouse on description below slider, it will react and change its color. I made it just for fun.

3. Open them all!

Main text content is hidden here!

3.1 That is it?

There are six dropdown elements on this site. You already know, that it can be opened by clicking on appropriate slide. Also you can click on any header, it will do the same.

3.2 How can I hide it now?

Just click on the button "Hide Text" and you will close article. This also works when it was opened from slider: you will close article and resume slider.

3.3 A link?

Yes, each article has a ink to the original page wich contain the information provided by this site. Even if it's only a course work, I think, it must be here.

4. Is everything OK?

And the last feature for today. I want to warn you about the fact, that this site was built for Chrome and tested in Chrome and Opera. If you have any problems with style or scripts, just try to open it in Chrome.

If your browser could not display all of site's feauters, you will receive a message about it in the footer of your page! Just check it, and will know that everything is ok!
